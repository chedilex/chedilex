Our Journey

Rooted in Batroun, Built for the Global Market

Established in 2021 by Eng. Tony El Chediac, in the historic city of Batroun, Lebanon, Chedilex Software Solutions began with a singular focus: to assemble top-tier Lebanese engineering talent and deliver world-class software that scales.

Over the years, we have evolved from a highly focused development team into a full-cycle software consultancy. We don't just take orders; we dive deep into your workflow, analyze systemic bottlenecks, and engineer custom platforms that optimize your day-to-day operations and fuel sustainable business growth.
